Intramuscular Stimulation

IMS & Gunn dry needling in Vancouver

When muscle pain won't let go, Intramuscular Stimulation (IMS) goes after the cause rather than the symptom. A fine acupuncture-style needle is placed into tight, shortened muscle bands to release them and settle down the nerves driving the tension.

A Vancouver technique. Gunn IMS was developed here in BC by Dr. Chan Gunn and is grounded in a neurological model — it targets muscles that have tightened because the nerve supplying them is irritated, which is why it can help pain that hasn't responded to stretching or massage alone.

What IMS can help with

  • Stubborn neck, shoulder and back tension that keeps coming back
  • Nerve-related muscle tightness and referred pain
  • Headaches with a muscular/neck component
  • Chronic tendon and overuse problems that have plateaued

What a session feels like

The needle itself is thin. When it reaches a tight band the muscle often gives a brief cramp or twitch — a normal response that signals the muscle releasing. Some muscle soreness for a day or two afterwards is common, and usually eases with gentle movement and heat.

IMS vs. acupuncture

They use similar needles but rest on different reasoning. Traditional acupuncture follows meridian points; IMS is guided by a physical exam of which muscles and nerve segments are involved, and the needle goes directly into those muscles. At Physiolab, IMS is delivered by physiotherapists certified in the technique, as one part of a broader treatment plan.

Is IMS covered by insurance?
When performed by a physiotherapist, IMS is generally billed as part of a physiotherapy visit and covered like other physio treatment under most extended-health and ICBC claims.
How many sessions will I need?
It varies with how long the problem has been there. Many people notice change within a few sessions; your physiotherapist will reassess as you go.
Is it the same as dry needling?
Gunn IMS is a specific, systematized form of dry needling with a neurological framework and a required certification.
