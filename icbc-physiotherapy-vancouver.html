ICBC physiotherapy in Vancouver — start within days, no referral needed

If you've been in a car accident in BC, ICBC's Enhanced Care covers physiotherapy from day one. You don't need a doctor's note to begin, and pre-approved visits are billed directly to ICBC.

The 12-week window matters. ICBC pre-approves treatment in the 12 weeks after your crash. Starting early — while tissues are still settling — generally gives the smoothest recovery, so it's worth booking sooner rather than waiting to "see if it settles."

What ICBC covers

  • No doctor's referral to start physiotherapy within 12 weeks of the accident.
  • Direct billing to ICBC for pre-approved visits — no large upfront payment.
  • A pre-set number of visits across physiotherapy, massage, chiropractic, acupuncture and active rehab (kinesiology).
  • Additional visits when there's clinical justification, requested on your behalf.

Coverage details change over time and depend on your claim — we'll confirm exactly what applies to you, including any user fee, before your first appointment.

Common car-accident injuries we treat

  • Whiplash & neck pain — the most common crash injury; stiffness, headaches and reduced range of motion.
  • Pinched-nerve symptoms — pain, tingling or numbness travelling into an arm or leg.
  • Low-back & mid-back pain from the impact and bracing.
  • Shoulder injuries from seatbelt loading and bracing on the wheel.
  • Concussion symptoms & dizziness — managed with vestibular rehabilitation.

How we approach ICBC recovery

An initial assessment maps which tissues are involved and what's driving your symptoms. From there it's hands-on manual therapy to calm things down, then a graded exercise and active-rehab plan to rebuild strength and confidence — with the aim of getting you back to driving, work and life, not just ticking off visits.

Do I need a doctor's referral?
No — you can start physiotherapy directly within 12 weeks of your accident.
How soon should I start?
As soon as you're able. Early, gentle movement usually settles whiplash and soft-tissue injuries faster than rest alone.
What if I already saw someone weeks ago?
You can still begin or resume treatment — bring your ICBC claim number and we'll sort out coverage.
